### 1.1

A

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \frac{1}{(1 - 1500 / 2500) + \frac{1500 / 2500}{1.5}} \\
    = 1.25
\end{aligned}
$$

B

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \frac{1}{(1 - 1500/2500) + \frac{1500 / 2500}{k}} = 1.67 \\
  \frac{1}{0.4 + \frac{0.6}{k}} = 1.67 \\
  \\
  k = \frac{1}{(1 / 1.67 - 0.4) / 0.6} = 3 \\
  \\
  v_{new} = 100 * 3 = 300 km/h
\end{aligned}
$$
